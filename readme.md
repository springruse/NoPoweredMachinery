# NoPowerMachinery
Adds a machinery table that runs at 50% at the cost of no power.

## Features
- Machinery table that operates with no power at 50% workspeed.
- Machining research unlocks early component making, at the cost of double resources and time on machinery tables.
- Microelectronics research unlocks advanced component making, at the cost of double resources and time on machinery tables.

## Installation
Download and extract to your RimWorld Mods folder.
